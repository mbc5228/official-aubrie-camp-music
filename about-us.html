ABOUT US
The beginning of Aubrie's singing career starts at a very young age. She has been making melodies since she
was an infant. Ever since she was young she always had the desire to pursue music as a career. Despite
the fact that this is a stereotypical dream for many young children,
the dream followed Aubrie into her teen years, and soon into adulthood. In an attempt to further
her relationship with music, Aubrie made the decision to audition for season 21 of ABC's
American Idol. She made it as far as Hollywood round, but then got cut during the duet round. Although neither her
audition or any of her performances aired, it was an opportunity for her to grow as a musician.
Since her time with American Idol, she has created social media accounts specifially for her music,
began booking gigs, and launched a website!
